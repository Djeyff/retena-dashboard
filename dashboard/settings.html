Why do some contacts need review?
WhatsApp uses internal IDs (LIDs) instead of phone numbers for privacy. For most contacts, Retena resolves them automatically. A small number — typically contacts saved under a nickname different from their phonebook name — can't be matched automatically. Only you know that "Coco" = "Corinne Jean". Enter their number below and it's resolved permanently — no further action needed.
